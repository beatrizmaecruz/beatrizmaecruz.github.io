MedScanner

Description

You know those little leafets that come with your medicine? MedScanner digitizes that information into a user-friendly web application. Everything on your over-the-counter medicine is just a barcode scan or text search away.

Context

This was my final year project, created within a group of 6.
I took on the responsibility of being a front-end developer and the UI/UX designer.

Our client wanted us to turn a government-approved medical resource, Medsafe (which contained pages of .pdfs of Consumer Medicine Information, aka the leaflets), into an accessible and user-friendly application. The ideal use of the application was for consumers to get easy access to in-depth information on their medicines prior to purchasing, to make better informed decisions.
