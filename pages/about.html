I was born in the Quezon City, Philippines! I immigrated to Auckland, New Zealand when I was a wee little 3-year-old Bea.

In 2022, I wrote my very-first piece of code in a compulsory university course. I quickly discovered that coding way too much fun for me. Ironically growing up, I had little to no interest in computers besides playing games (…fine, just Roblox) on them. But I always grew up around hardware scattered around the house since my dad works in IT.

I have a much longer history with the arts than I do with sciences.

  • I grew up watching cartoons and consider myself a life-long self-taught drawer. Nowadays, I do it mostly for fun nowadays when I feel the urge.
  • I learnt Photoshop in high school for **graphic design** and my love for it still goes strong! My Pinterest is full to the brim.
  • Halfway through my second year in university, I felt a strong urge to learn 3D modelling. I did a few courses in Blender and still do it every once in a while. However sometimes I just cheat and use world-building games...

So, what is Beatriz currently hyperfixated on?

Ah yes, my famous ever-revolving door of different interests.

As of this moment, I am currently obsessed with space, aerospace, astrophysics. This may or may-not be related to watching Star Wars and The Expanse.

  • If the moon, stars, or a plane is visible in the sky, I guarantee you I will marvel at it.
  • I’m in the process of researching and saving up for my first telescope!
